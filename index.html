ABOUT

Antonio Amodeo is a film director, screenwriter, producer, editor, and composer who currently resides in Canada. Antonio has been noted for his use of symbolism, fantasy, realism and blending of eclectic rock music with image. His body of work to date addresses such themes as identity, guilt, redemption, and machismo.

Along with Antonioâ€™s growing film work, he has also had a large involvement in the Canadian music industry. Antonio started his career as an entrepreneur, then moved into performance and composition. During his time in the music industry he was fortunate enough to work with Joel "deadmau5" Zimmerman and The Trews. Wanting a new challenge, Antonio left the industry in 2010 to pursue his first passion – film.
