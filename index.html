ABOUT


Antonio Amodeo is a film director, screenwriter, producer, editor, and composer who currently resides in Canada. Antonio has been noted for his use of symbolism, fantasy, realism and blending of eclectic rock music with image. His body of work to date addresses such themes as identity, guilt, redemption, and machismo.

Milk Bath

Milk Bath is a narrative short film that I edited for a young and upcoming film maker. This short looks at lactose intolerance on a humorous level.


Through the Eyes of Samuel

Through the Eyes of Samuel is a project film I edited. This film presented a number of challenges during the production process (camera problems, time and budget constraints). Despite the challenges, the end result is proved positive.


Screaming Tunnel

The Screaming Tunnel is a short documentary about a Canadian urban legend. The documentary investigates the myth and examines it through historical documentation and interviews.


Descending a Cadence - Director's Cut

Descending a Cadence is a project film shot in 2011. The guideline of the project was to document a process from start to finish within a time allowance of three minutes. Taking into consideration that a process film can be very one dimensional, an attempt was made to stylistically add a multi-level message into the film.


Diana

Diana is a short documentary that presents a foreign student's first few months in the Canadian university education system. Steering away from the classic interview, the documentary attempts to capture Diana's most candid moments while she prepares for her first English speaking presentation.


Romeo Quebec Romeo

Romeo Quebec Romeo is a short profile documentary that illustrates the profound determination and positive outlook of its subject. Shot in 2011, this short was a small budget project with a restricted time limit.


All Routine


All Routine is one of my very first project shorts in which I produced, directed and wrote. Though the narrative short is simplistic in nature, this was the first film which attempted to have 'production value' within the frame. Part of the project guideline was to do a visual representation of a Director that we admired. We attempted to emulate Martin Scorsese. You can be the judge as to whether that was successfully achieved.
